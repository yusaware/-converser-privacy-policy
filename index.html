Converser Dating App Assistant ("Converser," "we," "us,"

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the App

United States (including California), the European
Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and other jurisdictions.

By using Converser, you agree to the terms of this
Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use
the App.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1 INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE DIRECTLY

When you create an account and use Converser, you
may provide:

  a) Account Information:
     • Email address (for account creation and login)
     • Password (encrypted and managed by Firebase
       Authentication; we do not have access to your
       actual password)
     • Display name or nickname
     • Age
     • Sex / gender
     • Nationality
     • Occupation or field of work
     • Optional additional personal details you choose
       to share

  b) Dossier Content:
     • Names or nicknames of persons you add
     • Optional photographs of persons of interest
       (stored locally on your device only)
     • Personal notes about persons of interest
     • Conversation history you manually enter or import

  c) Communication Style Profile:
     • Responses you provide during the onboarding
       practice conversation, which are analyzed to
       understand your communication patterns. This
       profile is stored locally on your device.

2.2 INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY

When you use our AI-powered features (Generate Messages,
Analyze Person, Compile Memory), conversation text and
context you have entered into the App is temporarily
transmitted to our AI processing partner, OpenRouter AI
(openrouter.ai), for the sole purpose of generating the
requested AI response. This transmission is necessary
for the core functionality of the App.

We do not operate servers that permanently store this
transmitted content. OpenRouter AI processes this data
in accordance with its own Privacy Policy, available at
openrouter.ai/privacy.

2.3 PAYMENT INFORMATION

We do not collect or store your payment card details.
All payment processing for token purchases and
subscriptions is handled exclusively by RevenueCat
(revenuecat.com), operating through the Google Play
Store and Apple App Store. Purchase transaction records
(amounts, dates, product types) are stored locally on
your device for your reference.

2.4 INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect your precise GPS location
  • We do not access your device contacts
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not serve behavioral advertising
  • We do not sell your personal information
    to third parties for any purpose

3. HOW WE STORE YOUR DATA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

3.1 LOCAL DEVICE STORAGE (PRIMARY STORAGE)

The vast majority of your personal data is stored
exclusively on your own device using an encrypted
local database (SQLite with SQLCipher encryption).
This includes:

  • Your profile information (name, age, sex,
    nationality, occupation)
  • All Dossier entries and notes
  • All conversation history
  • All Memory logs
  • Your Communication Style Profile
  • Token balance and transaction history

Because this data lives on your device and only
on your device:

  • We cannot access it remotely
  • We cannot sell it
  • If you delete the App or lose your device,
    this data is permanently lost unless you
    have a device backup

3.2 FIREBASE AUTHENTICATION (AUTHENTICATION ONLY)

Your email address and authentication credentials
are stored by Google Firebase Authentication
(firebase.google.com), a third-party service we
use solely for account creation and login. Firebase
is a Google service and is subject to Google's
Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy).

We store in Firebase:
  • Your email address
  • Your hashed authentication credentials
    (we cannot see your actual password)
  • A unique account identifier (UID)

We do NOT store your conversations, dossier entries,
or any app content in Firebase.

3.3 TEMPORARY AI PROCESSING

When you use AI features, relevant conversation
context is temporarily sent to OpenRouter AI for
processing. This data is:
  • Transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Used only to generate the requested AI response
  • Not permanently stored by Converser on external
    servers after processing is complete
  • Subject to OpenRouter's own data handling
    practices (openrouter.ai/privacy)

       occupation)

     INFORMATION"

     We do not sell or share personal information
     as defined under CCPA/CPRA. You do not need

  i) HOW TO SUBMIT A CCPA/CPRA REQUEST

     To exercise any of the above rights, contact us:
     Email: yusaware.support@gmail.com
     Subject line: "California Privacy Rights Request"

     your verifiable consumer request. We may need

     your request.

7.1 LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR),
we process your personal data on the following
legal bases:

  a) PERFORMANCE OF CONTRACT (Article 6(1)(b)):
     Processing your account credentials and profile
     information is necessary to provide the App's
     services you have requested.

  b) CONSENT (Article 6(1)(a)):
     Where we ask for your consent (such as for
     optional additional profile details), you may
     withdraw that consent at any time by updating
     your profile or deleting your account.

  c) LEGITIMATE INTERESTS (Article 6(1)(f)):
     We process certain data to improve app
     performance and ensure security, where these
     interests are not overridden by your rights.

7.2 YOUR GDPR RIGHTS

As a data subject in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland,
you have the following rights:

  a) RIGHT OF ACCESS (Article 15)
     You have the right to obtain confirmation of
     whether we process your personal data and to
     access a copy of that data.

  b) RIGHT TO RECTIFICATION (Article 16)
     You have the right to have inaccurate personal
     data corrected. Update your profile in:
     Settings → Edit Profile

  c) RIGHT TO ERASURE / "RIGHT TO BE FORGOTTEN"
     (Article 17)
     You have the right to request deletion of your
     personal data where it is no longer necessary
     for the purposes for which it was collected.
     Delete your account via:
     Settings → Account → Delete Account

  d) RIGHT TO RESTRICTION OF PROCESSING (Article 18)
     You have the right to request that we restrict
     the processing of your personal data in certain
     circumstances.

  e) RIGHT TO DATA PORTABILITY (Article 20)
     You have the right to receive your personal data
     in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable
     format. Contact us to request a data export.

  f) RIGHT TO OBJECT (Article 21)
     You have the right to object to processing of
     your personal data based on our legitimate
     interests.

  g) RIGHTS RELATED TO AUTOMATED DECISION-MAKING
     The App does not make solely automated decisions
     that produce legal or similarly significant
     effects concerning you. AI-generated suggestions
     are presented as options for YOU to choose from
     and are not decisions made about you.

  • Local data is encrypted using SQLCipher

However, no method of electronic transmission or
storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use
commercially acceptable means to protect your
personal information, we cannot guarantee its
absolute security.
